Gym App Project Details

As an avid gym enjoyer I think there is a severe lack of information regarding exercises on the internet. The information is there but it is very scattered. You have to search up the exercise, read a wiki page and then look it up on youtube to see how its done. This app takes it and puts it all on one digestable location. You search the site, it gives you what muscles it targets, it shows you videos so you can do it, and then it reccomends different related exercises to work those muscles even more. On top of all that it gives you equipment reccomendations in case you don't have what's needed or if you want to save time doing things on the same machine.
